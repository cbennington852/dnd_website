## 1. Quick draw
Add half your firearms stat to your dex when rolling quickdraw. 
Example: 
80% Firearms
50% Dextarity
~(90%) Quick Draw

## 2. Base Level up 
+10% (2 pts) to any base stat. Example STR, CON, DEX...

## 3. Lethal Burst
Add +10% lethality to all firearms rolls. 

## 4. Killer Instincts
* Killing monsters results in a 1d6 sanity gain
* Monsters surviving a fight with your charicter results in a 1d6 sanity loss.

## 5. Trained Shooter
Aiming bonus is now +40% (was 20% previously)

## 6. Burst fire
Once per session, shoot twice on a single turn.
