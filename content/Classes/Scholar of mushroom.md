
## 1. Dive into the madness
+50% to unnatural. Your willpower can be reduced to 1 before you feel any effects. 

* Note: If you already have unnatural this does not go above 65% 
* Note: this will reduce your sanity capacity to your unnatural.

## 3. Feel Good Fuzziness
* Saving an innocent person restores 1d10+2 of sanity
* Accidentally killing an innocent person is an extra 1d6 of sanity loss.
